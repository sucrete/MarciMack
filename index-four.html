The Club at
MariMack

Fall in love with the greatest game ever played at the MariMack Golf Complex in Kearney, Missouri! We are an 18-hole executive golf course located 25 minutes northeast of downtown Kansas City.

Scenic Fairways and Small-Town Hospitality

MariMack is a privately-owned, public golf course. While we do offer memberships, they are not required to visit our facility. Our course, driving range, and sports bar are open to the general public.
