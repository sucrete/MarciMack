Book Tee Time

If you have a minimum of two players, you may use this to book online. This is always for 18 holes + cart throughout the day. We do set rates for one hour before sundown that are intended for 9 holes + cart (or as many holes as you can get in before sundown).

Member ID: Located on your Member ID Card below the barcode on back. Exactly how it appears: All caps, no spaces with letters & numbers. (i.e.: "SMITH001-059")
